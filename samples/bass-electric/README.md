# Electric bass note samples

Fingered ("reg") samples of the **darkblack** 5-string from **Black And Blue
Basses** by Karoryfer Samples (2023 KVR Developer Challenge entry).
Source: https://github.com/sfzinstruments/karoryfer.black-and-blue-basses

License: **CC0 1.0**.

Flattened subset: sounding roots a minor third apart (E1–E3, MIDI 28–52 — the
source names/keycenters are written an octave above sounding pitch, verified
by measuring the audio), two velocity layers (mp/f of four), four round-robins
each, peak-normalized as one group, trimmed to 6s, mono MP3. Files are
`m<midi>_<layer>_rr<n>.mp3`; `manifest.json` maps roots to layers, in-between
notes retune at playback (max ±1.5 semitones).

Selected via the Bass "Samples" switch in the Player's Mixer (the Pop, Indie
Pop, and Funk genre presets choose it automatically).
