# Upright piano note samples

Sustain samples of the **Knight upright piano** from the Versilian Community
Sample Library (VCSL), sampled by Simon Dalzell of Ivy Audio for the VSCO 2
Pro set. Source: https://github.com/sgossner/VCSL

License: **CC0 1.0**.

Flattened subset: whole-tone sampled roots covering C#2–F4 (MIDI 37–77),
two velocity layers, peak-normalized as one group, trimmed to 5s with a
fade-out, and encoded to MP3. Files are named `p<midi>_vl<layer>.mp3`;
`manifest.json` maps roots to layers, and in-between notes retune at playback
(max ±1 semitone).
