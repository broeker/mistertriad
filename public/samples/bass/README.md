# Upright bass note samples

Pizzicato samples from **Meatbass** by Karoryfer Samples — a 1958 Otto Rubner
double bass played by Drogomir Smolken, recorded by Ludwik Zamenhof.
Source: https://github.com/sfzinstruments/karoryfer.meatbass

License: **CC0 1.0** ("royalty-free for all commercial and non-commercial use").

This is a flattened subset of the full library: sampled roots a minor third
apart (Eb1–C3), two velocity layers (vl2/vl3 of four), four round-robins each,
peak-normalized as one group and encoded to MP3. `manifest.json` maps MIDI
root notes to their velocity layers and round-robin files; notes in between
sampled roots are retuned at playback via `playbackRate` (max ±1.5 semitones).
