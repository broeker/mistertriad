# Drum kit samples

From the **Salamander Drumkit** by Alexander Holm — an acoustic kit
(18x18" kick, 14x5" birch-stave snare, Stagg 14" SH hi-hat) recorded through
overhead mics. Source: https://archive.org/details/SalamanderDrumkit

License: the original 2010 release shipped as CC-BY-SA 3.0; the author later
placed the kit in the **public domain** (announcement on
https://rytmenpinne.wordpress.com/sounds-and-such/salamander-drumkit/ dated
2022-03-04: "this is now public domain! Have fun with it, it's yours and
noones!"). Attribution kept here out of courtesy.

This is a flattened subset: kick/snare/hat/toms, two velocity layers each,
4–5 round-robins per layer, peak-normalized per piece and encoded to MP3.
`manifest.json` maps piece names to layers/round-robins plus a `trim` mix
level.

The `brush_*` files are brushed snare hits from **Swirly Drums** by Karoryfer
Samples (CC0), https://shop.karoryfer.com/pages/free-samples — soft (vl4) and
accent (vl9) layers, top mic. The stomp sound remains synthesized in
`src/audio.js`.
